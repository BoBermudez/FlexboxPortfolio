About Me

I'm originally from Lompoc, a small town on the central coast of California. After graduating high school, I moved to Oregon to attend college and enjoy all of the outdoor activities that Oregon has to offer.


In college, I had planned to major in computer science, but as an out-of-state student, tuition became a difficult hurdle to overcome. Thankfully, discovering Thinkful has helped me restart my path to becoming a web developer. Most of my past jobs in the service industry have made me appreciate and look forward to working in a team setting to tackle a project or task!


When I'm not working, I can be found doing some of my favorite activities such as cooking vegan food (my favorite source for recipes is Bosh,) practicing yoga, hiking throughout the Pacific Northwest and taking photos during my outdoor activities.
